ASG4 Lighting: Notes to Grader -

Hello!

(1) My cube is part of my blocky animal.

(2) The sphere is directly behind my blocky animal.

(3) The moving cube is the lighting (ambient+diffuse+specular), and you can changes its position using the sliders. I would recommend changing the sliders so that Y is in the middle and Z is slighting above the middle of the bar for the best effect.

(4) You can press the On/Off buttons to turn the ighting on and off.

(5) Visual marker of light location(s): the two cubes

(6) The spotlight moves over time, and you can also adjust the light manually by using the x/y/z sliders.

(7) I have a spotlight near the neck and wings of the bird. It's easiest to see the pink-ish hue of the spotlight shining at the sphere if you move the other light to middle-Y and middle-Z position.

Thank you!
